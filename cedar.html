How to Reach Us

From Airport:

Cedar Inn is located 13 km from Srinagar International Airport. You can book a prepaid taxi or request our airport pickup service for a comfortable journey to the hotel.

By Car/Taxi:

Located in Rajbagh, near Modern Hospital. From city center (Lal Chowk), head towards Rajbagh. The hotel is easily accessible and well-signposted in the neighborhood.

Local Transport:

Auto-rickshaws and local taxis are readily available. The hotel is approximately 3 km from the Tourist Reception Center and 2 km from Dal Lake.
